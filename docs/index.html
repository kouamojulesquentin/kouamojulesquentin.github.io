About Me

I’m a PhD Student in TIMA lab, working on Architectures and Software Methods for Mixed System Testing.

Experience

Since November 2023: Phd Student

CNRS - Grenoble Inp - TIMA

The thesis focuses on the integration of digital and analog testing methodologies for mixed-signal systems, which are becoming increasingly common in modern circuits. Currently, the testing methods for digital and analog components are separate and distinct, despite their close link in operation. The goal of the thesis is to develop a unified approach that bridges the technological and methodological gap between the digital and analog domains, allowing for the development of hybrid software and hardware solutions optimized for designers’ needs.

Supervisors: Emmanuel SIMEU - Michele PORTOLAN
